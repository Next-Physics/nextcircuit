Research Objective:
Investigate the mechanisms and efficacy of oncolytic viruses in cancer therapy.

Key Areas of Interest:
- Mechanisms of action of oncolytic viruses.
- Efficacy of oncolytic virus therapy in specific cancer types.
- Safety and side effects associated with oncolytic viruses.
- Case studies and clinical trial outcomes related to oncolytic viruses.
- Combination therapies involving oncolytic viruses.

Target Publication Types:
- Peer-reviewed articles
- Preprints from BioRxiv
- Review articles or meta-analyses
